Careers

We currently have positions available and are looking for developers, postdocs, and students interested in working with our Parsl team.

We are located at the University of Chicago's Hyde Park Campus out of the Computation Institute (CI). The CI is a joint initiative between The University of Chicago and Argonne National Laboratory with a mission to advance science through innovative computational approaches. It serves as an intellectual nexus, bringing together researchers from different disciplines with common interests in advancing the state-of- the-art in computing and its applications. The CI is also a resource center for those building and applying advanced computational platforms for science, providing expert assistance to scholars whose work requires the most advanced computational methods.

We offer competitive packages with great benefits and flexible work hours.

To find out more information or express your interest contact Kyle Chard.
