Support

If you are using Parsl or planning to try it, join our parsl-project Slack channel or subscribe to the parsl-user email list to hear what other users are doing, ask questions, and listen in on and join the community discussion.

If you'd like to contribute as a developer, or participate in the discussion of implementation issues, please follow the project on GitHub.
